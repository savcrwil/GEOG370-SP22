State Distribution of US Public School Teachers (2012)

This map depicts the 2012 distribution of US public school (PS) teachers per state. Some states spend more on their public schools than others, and I wondered if this would be reflected in the number of teachers in each state. I normalized this data by dividing the number of PS teachers per state by the total number of PS teachers in the US. Clearly, states with larger populations have a larger percentage of teachers, but there seem to be some discrepancies among mid-sized states. The most recent data I could find was from 2012, but I think it would be interesting to compare this to post-COVID teacher numbers. White states on the map have no data.
